The Xiaomi 15 Ultra is Xiaomi’s top-tier powerhouse designed for enthusiasts who want everything a modern smartphone can deliver from pro-level photography to blazing performance. Built around a striking 6.73-inch WQHD+ LTPO AMOLED display with up to 120 Hz adaptive refresh rate and extremely high peak brightness, it ensures vibrant visuals and smooth interaction in all conditions.
Under the hood, the phone is powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Elite chipset, paired with fast LPDDR5X RAM and UFS 4.1 storage, delivering exceptional speed for gaming, multitasking, and AI-accelerated tasks.
Where the 15 Ultra really stands out is its quad-camera system co-engineered with Leica, featuring a large 1-inch main sensor, an advanced 200 MP periscope telephoto lens, plus ultra-wide and additional telephoto optics giving users professional-grade photography and versatile zoom performance. Its video capabilities include 4K recording at 120 fps, Dolby Vision support, and strong stabilization, making it a compelling choice for creators.
The device also packs a substantial battery with fast wired and wireless charging, robust connectivity features (including Wi-Fi 7 and Bluetooth 6.0), IP68 water-dust resistance, and runs Xiaomi’s HyperOS 2.0 on Android 15 for a fluid and customizable software experience.
