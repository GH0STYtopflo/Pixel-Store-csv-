The CMF Phone 2 Pro is a standout mid‑range Android smartphone from CMF by Nothing that delivers a strong mix of performance, visuals, and battery life at an accessible price point. It sports a large 6.77‑inch FHD+ AMOLED display with a smooth 120 Hz refresh rate, offering vibrant colors and fluid motion for everyday use and media consumption. Under the hood, it’s powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 7300 Pro chipset paired with 8 GB of RAM, providing capable performance for multitasking and everyday apps. 
Photography is versatile with a triple‑camera setup featuring a 50 MP main sensor, a 50 MP telephoto with 2× optical zoom, and an 8 MP ultra‑wide lens, while the 16 MP front camera handles selfies and video calls well. A 5,000 mAh battery backed by 33 W fast charging keeps the device going throughout the day, and the phone runs Nothing OS 3.2 based on Android for a clean, minimal software experience. 
With its striking design, 5G support, and long‑term software update promise, the CMF Phone 2 Pro aims to offer excellent value for users looking for a capable device without flagship pricing.
